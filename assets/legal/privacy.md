## 1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to run the game, save your progress, and power online features such as leaderboards and tournaments.

### Account information

- If you sign in with **email**, we collect your email address.
- If you sign in with **Google**, we receive your name, email address, and basic profile information from your Google account.
- If you play as a **guest**, we generate an anonymous account identifier so your progress can be saved. No personal details are required.

### Gameplay data

- Scores, runs, waves reached, kill counts, and other game statistics.
- Win streaks, daily challenge progress, and achievements.
- Leaderboard and tournament entries, including the display name shown on boards.
- Your in-game coin balance and the cosmetic and loadout selections you make.

### Technical data

- Authentication tokens used to keep you signed in.
- A device identifier and basic diagnostic information provided by the services we use (see Section 4) to keep your account secure.

We do **not** collect your precise location, contacts, photos, or microphone data, and we do **not** sell your personal information to anyone.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information above to:

- Create and secure your account and keep you signed in.
- Save your progress and synchronise it across your devices.
- Display leaderboards, tournaments, and your ranking.
- Operate the in-game economy: coins, the Gunsmith perks, and the Outfitter cosmetics.
- Detect and prevent cheating, fraud, and abuse.
- Diagnose crashes and improve the game.

---

## 3. How Your Information Is Stored

- Your progress is stored **locally on your device** so the game works offline.
- A copy of your account and online progress is stored on our secure backend server so it can be restored and used for leaderboards and tournaments.
- We retain your data for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account (see Section 6), your associated data is deleted.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third-party services, which process limited data on our behalf:

- **Google Firebase Authentication** — to create and manage sign-in (email, Google, and guest accounts).
- **Google Play Services** — for app distribution and platform features.

These providers process your data under their own privacy policies, including Google at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

We do not integrate third-party advertising networks, and the App does not show ads.
